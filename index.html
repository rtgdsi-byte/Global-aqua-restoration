RT

Ross Trevino

Chief Executive Officer & Chairman

Ross Trevino is an international business development executive with decades of experience creating sales opportunities, building strategic partnerships, and opening executive-level doors across Asia, the Middle East, South America, Central America, the United States, Canada, Brazil, Panama, and the Caribbean.

Throughout his career, Ross has worked across multiple industries, including water restoration technologies, advanced battery technology, pharmaceuticals and skincare, produce and juice products, telecommunications, and mergers and acquisitions. However, few opportunities have meant more to him than helping advance environmental solutions through Global Aqua Restoration.

Roberto Barbuci

Senior Business Advisor

Roberto Barbuci is a Brazilian attorney and business executive with more than 25 years of experience in the water and sanitation sector, infrastructure concessions, and public-private partnerships. He has worked with leading sanitation companies in Brazil, including Iguá Saneamento, with experience in strategic expansion, operational efficiency, and large-scale investments that deliver clean water and wastewater services.

As Senior Business Advisor to Global Aqua Restoration, Roberto provides strategic guidance on business development, government relations, concessions, financing, and partnerships throughout Brazil and international markets.

Luis Machado brings more than 20 years of commercial leadership and international business development experience, including a decade as Commercial Director at Orbe Brasil.
