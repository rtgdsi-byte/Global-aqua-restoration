About Global Aqua Restoration

A veteran-led company focused on water restoration and sustainable environmental solutions.

Global Aqua Restoration was founded on a simple belief: clean water is essential for healthier communities, stronger economies, and a sustainable planet.

We develop and deliver proprietary low-pH water restoration technologies that support municipalities, utilities, agriculture, industries, and environmental restoration projects. Our work is built around science, responsible deployment, and local partnerships.

Our Technology

Proprietary low-pH water restoration technology engineered for practical field performance.

Our technology is designed to support water quality improvement, odor control, wastewater challenges, agriculture, lagoons, lakes, reservoirs, and industrial water applications without publicly disclosing confidential chemistry, dosage, or formulation details.

Leadership Team

Experienced leaders with global business, finance, regional execution, and environmental engineering expertise.

Ross Trevino

Founder & Chief Executive Officer

Ross Trevino is an American entrepreneur, U.S. Army veteran, and international business executive with more than 30 years of experience in business development, mergers and acquisitions, executive sales, capital raising, and strategic partnerships.

After graduating from university, Ross opened several businesses early in his career in the United States before moving to Canada, where he opened and developed more than 40 business locations.

Since 2014, Ross has worked extensively in Brazil, building relationships with business leaders, government representatives and officials, municipalities, chiefs of police, fire service leadership, military officials, public safety organizations, and strategic partners. He has also worked with and been represented by some of Brazil’s largest law firms and specialized boutique firms.

Ross is passionate about humanitarian and environmental projects that help communities, improve water quality, and support sustainable growth. Ross resides in Florida, USA.

Edigimar Antonio Maximiliano Jr. “Max”

Director of Investor Relations

Max is a senior finance and investment banking executive with more than 35 years of experience in capital markets, mergers and acquisitions, corporate finance, treasury, and investor relations.

He began his career at ING Barings trading emerging-market sovereign debt bonds, later served in treasury leadership with General Motors in Brazil and New York, and held senior roles with Iguatemi Group, Itaú Unibanco, Bradesco BBI, Safra Bank, and XP/Oriz.

As Director of Investor Relations, Max supports institutional communications, capital markets strategy, investor relationships, and corporate finance discipline for Global Aqua Restoration.

Luis Machado

General Manager – South America

Luis Machado is a Brazilian business executive with more than 30 years of experience in business development, commercial leadership, and international technology projects.

He has held senior leadership positions with Motorola Solutions, Ericsson, and Rontan Group, leading strategic initiatives across telecommunications, transportation, public safety, critical infrastructure, and technology-driven business development.

Fluent in Portuguese, English, and Spanish, Luis leads regional growth, commercial operations, and strategic partnerships across South America.

Mauricio R. Martinez, M.S.

Technical Advisor & Environmental Engineer

Mauricio R. Martinez is a biochemical and environmental engineer with decades of experience in water treatment, wastewater systems, environmental restoration, and municipal water infrastructure.

His background includes work connected to Lake Texcoco, Mexican water institutions, wastewater treatment design, stabilization lagoons, environmental engineering, and technical support for water restoration projects in Mexico, Vietnam, and Malaysia.
