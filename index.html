About Global Aqua Restoration

A veteran-led company focused on water restoration and sustainable environmental solutions.

Global Aqua Restoration was founded on a simple belief: clean water is essential for healthier communities, stronger economies, and a sustainable planet.

We develop and deliver proprietary low-pH water restoration technologies that support municipalities, utilities, agriculture, industries, and environmental restoration projects. Our work is built around science, responsible deployment, and local partnerships.

Our Technology

Proprietary low-pH water restoration technology engineered for practical field performance.

Our technology is designed to support water quality improvement, odor control, wastewater challenges, agriculture, lagoons, lakes, reservoirs, and industrial water applications without publicly disclosing confidential chemistry, dosage, or formulation details.

Leadership Team

Experienced leaders with global business, finance, regional execution, and environmental engineering expertise.

Ross Trevino

Founder & Chief Executive Officer

Ross Trevino is an American entrepreneur, U.S. Army veteran, and international business executive with more than 30 years of experience in business development, mergers and acquisitions, executive sales, capital raising, and strategic partnerships. After graduating from university, Ross opened several businesses in the United States before moving to Canada, where he opened and developed more than 40 locations.

Since 2014, he has worked extensively in Brazil, developing relationships with business leaders, municipalities, government and public-safety officials, military leadership, and strategic partners. He has also worked with leading Brazilian law firms and specialized boutique firms. Ross leads Global Aqua Restoration's international growth and is passionate about humanitarian and environmental projects. He resides in Florida, USA.

Edigimar Antonio Maximiliano Jr. “Max”

Director of Investor Relations

Edigimar Antonio Maximiliano Jr., known as Max, is a senior finance and investment banking executive with more than 35 years of experience in capital markets, mergers and acquisitions, corporate finance, treasury, and investor relations. He began his career at ING Barings trading emerging-market sovereign debt bonds and later held treasury leadership positions with General Motors Corporation in Brazil and New York.

Max subsequently served in senior financial and investment-banking roles with the Iguatemi Group, Itaú Unibanco, Bradesco BBI, Safra Bank, and XP/Oriz. His background includes executive leadership of brokerage firms affiliated with Safra, XP/Oriz, and Bradesco. At Global Aqua Restoration, he supports institutional communications, capital-markets strategy, investor relationships, and corporate-finance discipline.

Luis Machado

General Manager – South America

Luis Machado is a Brazilian business executive with more than 30 years of experience in business development, commercial leadership, and international technology projects. He has held senior positions with Motorola Solutions, Ericsson, and the Rontan Group, leading strategic initiatives in telecommunications, transportation, public safety, critical infrastructure, rail transportation, and urban mobility.

Fluent in Portuguese, English, and Spanish, Luis has extensive experience developing new markets, managing strategic partnerships, and working with public and private organizations throughout Brazil and Latin America. As General Manager – South America, he leads Global Aqua Restoration's regional expansion and partnerships with municipalities, utilities, industries, universities, and environmental organizations.

Mauricio R. Martínez, M.S.

Technical Advisor & Environmental Engineer

Mauricio R. Martínez is a biochemical and environmental engineer with decades of experience in water treatment, wastewater systems, environmental restoration, and municipal water infrastructure. His academic background includes advanced studies in systems and environmental engineering, with specialization in water treatment and industrial applications.

Mauricio provides scientific guidance, technical evaluation, and engineering support for Global Aqua Restoration's international projects. He has worked with the company's proprietary low-pH water-restoration formula in Mexico, Vietnam, and Malaysia, and brings practical field experience involving treatment design, stabilization lagoons, odor control, environmental remediation, and sustainable water-management solutions.
